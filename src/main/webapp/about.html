Since its grand opening in April 2014, Chengdu Gourmet (previously named as chengdugourmet in Squirrel Hill) has dedicated to its mission to introduce authentic Sichuan cuisine, in both tradition and up-to-date style, to the tribune area. Sichuan cuisube us a style of Chinese cuisine originating in the Sichuan Province of southwestern China famed for flavors.

Chengdu Gourmet was opening in 2014 and its mission is to provide authentic Chinese(Chengdu) cuisine to the residence of the city of Pittsburgh. The flavor that Chengdu Gourmet provides is a style of Chinese cuisine originating in the Sichuan Province of southwestern China. The executive chef/Owner, Wei Zhu, has been living in Chengdu for decades and has been cooking authentic Sichuan style food for more than 30 years.

Pittsburgh top 10 Restaurant twice in 2015 and 2016, and it is the only Chinese restaurant that listed among all the candidates. the Chef Wei Zhu has never made our customers disappointed with cooking the most authentic Sichuan food in the Pittsburgh area. We have more than 1500 sf dinning area and a 200 sf party room which is capable of accommodating up to 20 guests. The restaurant have become an ideal choice for wedding parties, graduation celebrations, business meetings and other personal or commercial gatherings. In 2017, we will continue to provide our customers with delicious food and quality services.

老四川, ChenduGourmet Gourmet，开业于2014年，是一家以经营正宗川菜为主的餐厅， 坐落在繁华的Squirrel Hill商业区。餐馆所有人，同时也是餐馆主厨的Wei Zhu先生从事于餐饮行业长达30年之久。

我们的餐馆已经连续两年(2015, 2016)被匹兹堡当地杂志评为匹兹堡十大餐馆之一，并且作为唯一的中餐馆入围最终的角逐。餐馆严格秉承传统川菜的特色，一菜一格，百菜百味, 烹饪以麻、辣、鲜、香为特色。皆在赋予匹兹堡地区无论华人还是本地人一个真实的四川美食的享受。
